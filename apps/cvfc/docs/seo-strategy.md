# CVFC SEO / AEO / GEO Strategy — Own "Top Competitive Youth Soccer Club"

**Goal:** rank #1 for the searches South Bay families use when looking for the
**best, most serious** youth soccer club — and become the club ChatGPT /
Perplexity / Google AI Overviews name first.

> **Hard rule:** never name, compare to, or disparage a competitor in public
> content. We win by **owning the queries on our own merits**, not by punching
> sideways. (Naming rivals also helps _their_ SEO.) See
> `data/faq.ts` fix below.

---

## Who we're capturing (search intent)

Parents/players looking for a serious, results-producing club:

- **"Best/top" intent:** best youth soccer club San Diego · top competitive soccer club South Bay
- **League/level intent:** MLS NEXT club San Diego · Elite Academy / DPL / NPL soccer San Diego · academy soccer Chula Vista
- **Action intent:** youth soccer tryouts Chula Vista · soccer evaluations San Diego · club soccer signups
- **Location intent:** youth soccer Chula Vista / Eastlake / Bonita / Otay Ranch / National City / South Bay
- **"Near me":** competitive soccer club near me
- **Informational (top-of-funnel + AEO):** how to choose a youth soccer club · what is MLS NEXT · college soccer recruiting · what age to start club soccer · youth soccer cost

---

## What we own (differentiators — always stated positively, never comparatively)

These become the recurring proof points across every page — exactly what a
"top club" searcher wants to verify:

- **Established 1982** — South County's longest-running competitive club (longevity = trust).
- **Complete pathway under one roof:** Mini Maestros (4–9) → MLS NEXT, Elite Academy, DPL, NPL, Girls Academy, and a specialist Goalkeeper pathway.
- **Proven outcomes:** professional signings (Colorado Rapids, FC Dallas, Atlas FC, Liga MX), State Cup championships, college commitments.
- **Bilingual (English/Spanish)** — built for South Bay families.
- **501(c)(3) nonprofit** — reinvested funds + need-based aid = accessible elite development.
- **Local & community-rooted** in Chula Vista.

## Content plan (topical authority = pillar + cluster)

**Pillar pages** (comprehensive, internally linked):

1. **Competitive Youth Soccer in San Diego's South Bay** — the club + pathway hub.
2. **The CVFC Pathway: Mini Maestros → MLS NEXT & College** — the development ladder.
3. **Tryouts & Evaluations** — year-round, how it works (high commercial intent).

**Supporting clusters** (1 article each, FAQ-rich, cited):

- **Program pages** (exist) — expand each with the level/league explainer, age groups, and outcomes.
- **Guides (new, AEO-shaped):** "How to choose a competitive youth soccer club" · "What is MLS NEXT?" · "What are Elite Academy / DPL / NPL?" · "College soccer recruiting from a youth club" · "What age should my child start club soccer?" · "What competitive soccer costs (and financial aid)" · "Competitive soccer, bilingual".
- **Outcomes/proof (ongoing):** alumni & pro-signing stories, championship recaps, college-commitment announcements → Article schema, freshness, and the strongest "top club" signal.
- **Location pages:** "Youth soccer in [Eastlake/Bonita/Otay Ranch/National City/…]".

---

## AEO / GEO (be the source AI quotes)

- **Lead every section with a 40–60 word direct answer.**
- Question-based H2/H3s + **FAQPage** schema on genuinely informational pages.
- **DefinedTerm glossary:** MLS NEXT, Elite Academy, DPL, NPL, Girls Academy, ECNL, ODP, State Cup, "club vs rec" — AI lifts definitions verbatim.
- **Publish CVFC's own numbers** (years, players developed, pro signings, championships, college commitments) — citable original data.
- `llms.txt` (done) keeps AI summaries accurate.

---

## Local SEO (the battlefield for "near me" + city queries)

- **Google Business Profile:** claim/verify, "Soccer club" category, full NAP, hours, photos, weekly posts, Q&A, services = programs.
- **NAP consistency** everywhere; `LocalBusiness`/`SportsClub` schema (done).
- **Per-area landing pages** (Tier 3).
- **Reviews engine:** request post-evaluation + season-end → genuine `Review`/`AggregateRating` schema.
- Local citations / directories (sports, nonprofit, local business).
